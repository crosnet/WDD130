About Us

Crosby techsupport has been in existence since 2010, offering a variety of tech services to our client in various locations. We have our headquarters based in Ghana, Accra and have other locations in other parts of the country.

We have fully qualified technicians who are ready to solve all your tech problems. Our solution plan include remote assistance service, which help us to provide our support services online as well.

We make sure small business get the full computer support they need in order to keep their business running daily. We set up their computer systems and provide the support along the line.

Have you ever wondered why you face network problems and how it gets resolved? We research potential threats on the networks and provide security solutions to protect your network from cyber-attacks and solve the problems that arises along the way.

Our services are offered for almost all kinds of computer devices (computers, mobile devices, network devices etc.) We also provide secondary services, which include software development, wed design and development, graphic design, training etc.
